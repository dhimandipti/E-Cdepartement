Cultural club

Talent Unleashed: Cultural Delights

Cultural club  is responsible for all the cultural events in the Institute around the year. Cultural club strives to bring out the hidden talent of the students of the college. It is the place where students can showcase what they have got. It helps to discover their talent. Cultural events include competitions such as instrumental, singing, dance etc. Every year this club organizes activities such as Talent Hunt, Fresher’s Party, Farewell, TWASK and Alumni Meet. Cultural club also celebrates festivals such as  Republic Day, Teacher’s day ,Yoga day Environment day etc which creates a vibrant atmosphere.

But everyone needs some time off, to relax and recharge. Cultural Club  is the best place for this.



Aim and objective

  • Showcasing diverse talents and promoting artistic expression.

  • Creating a vibrant cultural atmosphere through festivals and events.

  • Nurturing creativity and exploring various art forms.

  • Bringing students together to celebrate and appreciate cultural diversity.

Events

Photography contest

On October 5, 2022, a "photography competition" was organised by the Cultural Club of the Electronic and Communication Engineering Department. The theme of the photography competition was "Nature." The cultural club's president ran the competition online. This competition aimed to develop photography skills and the ability to critically examine angles of nature among the students. Thirty students participated with full zeal in this competition. The photographs taken by the students were striking.

The pictures were judged by the faculty of the ECE department, Asst. Prof. Manvi Sharma and Asst. Prof. Pooja Sharma. After the completion of this competition, winners were announced and given prizes.

Winners::

  1. Zubair Ahmad Sofi (6th sem)

  2. Adarsh Guleria (6th sem)

  3. Ayush (4th sem)

Mehndi contest

On 12 October 2022 Mehndi Competition was organised by the cultural club of the department. Students of Electronics and Communication department participated in the event with great enthusiasm and zeal. They created beautiful design and showcased their artistic skills. Prof. Himanshu Monga. HOD ECE, encouraged and appreciated the student and the department for putting in efforts to keep this cultural and traditional art form alive. 

Winners::

  1. Priyanka (6th sem)

  2. Shivanshi (6th sem)

  3. Ishita (4th sem)

Singing contest

On 18 April 2023 Singing Competition was organised by the cultural club of the department. Student of E.C.E , ME & C.S.E department participated in the event.

Everyone gave a brilliant and mind-blowing performance. Everyone enjoy a lot. Prof. Nitasha bisht HOD E.C.E and Asst.Prof. Pooja Sharma were the judges of that competition. At the end of the competition, both of the teachers finalize the talented students & winners were announced and given prizes.

Winners::

  1. Deeraj (4th sem ECE)

  2. Tushar (6th sem ECE)

  3. Aditya (4th sem ME)
